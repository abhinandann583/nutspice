Frequently Asked Questions

Are your products vegan?

Our Peri Peri and Pudina Burst flavors are 100% vegan. The Cream & Onion and Cheesy flavors contain dairy derivatives.

Are they gluten-free?

Yes! All our makhana flavors are completely gluten-free and safe for those with gluten sensitivities.

What is the shelf life?

Unopened, our premium jars have a shelf life of 6 months. Once opened, we recommend consuming within 14 days and keeping the lid tightly closed.
